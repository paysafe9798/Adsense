About Shani Store

Shani Store is a Free Fire gaming services brand focused on building simple, fast tools for the Free Fire community. Our flagship tool, the FF Emote Panel, lets players send in-game emotes directly to a lobby using just a UID and team code — no downloads, no installs, just a browser.

What We Do

We build lightweight web tools for Free Fire players, including:

  • The Emote Panel — send emotes instantly to any active lobby
  • Guides and tips for getting the most out of Free Fire features
  • Bot hosting tools under our Shani Hosting sub-brand

Why We Built This

Sending emotes and interacting with lobbies in Free Fire can be clunky through official channels. We wanted a fast, no-friction panel that works from any phone browser, so players can spend less time fiddling with tools and more time playing.

Who's Behind It

Shani Store is run independently, with all tools built, tested, and maintained in-house. We're active in the Free Fire community and build based on what players actually ask for.

A Note on Safety

We always recommend using third-party tools like ours at your own discretion, and we do our best to keep the Service reliable, ad-supported, and free to use.
