Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Shani Store ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Free Fire Emote Panel website (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Emote Panel, we may process the following information:

  • Free Fire Player UID and Team Code that you voluntarily enter to use the emote-sending feature
  • Basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information, collected automatically for security and analytics purposes
  • Usage logs (timestamps, actions taken) used to prevent abuse and keep the Service running smoothly

We do not require account registration to use the core emote panel, and we do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information such as real names, addresses, or payment details through this Service.

2. Cookies and Advertising

We use Google Analytics and may use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this website. Google and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide relevant advertisements.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses data at Google's Partner Sites policy.

3. How We Use Information

  • To operate and maintain the emote-sending functionality
  • To detect, prevent, and block abusive or fraudulent use (e.g. UID blocking)
  • To improve site performance and user experience
  • To display relevant advertising through third-party ad networks

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited technical data with trusted third-party service providers (such as hosting providers, analytics, and advertising networks) strictly to operate the Service, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

Usage logs are retained only for a short period for abuse prevention and are not used for any other purpose. UIDs entered into the emote panel are not permanently stored beyond what is required for the Service to function.

6. Children's Privacy

This Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings and opt out of personalized ads via the links above. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the Service.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page.
